## WHY THESE FOODS MATTER (Brain Recovery Science)

**Omega-3 (DHA/EPA):**
DHA is the primary structural fat in neuronal membranes. Sub-concussive impacts damage these membranes. Dietary omega-3 provides raw material for repair. Sources: oily fish, fish oil supplements, walnuts, chia seeds.

**Blueberries:**
Anthocyanins cross the blood-brain barrier directly. Reduce neuroinflammation. Amen's #1 recommended brain food. Daily consumption is ideal.

**Eggs (whole):**
Richest dietary source of choline → acetylcholine (the memory neurotransmitter). Yolk is essential — never egg whites only.

**Dark leafy greens (spinach, kale):**
Folate, magnesium, nitrates. Nitrates convert to nitric oxide → vasodilation → increased cerebral blood flow.

**Turmeric + black pepper:**
Curcumin is anti-neuroinflammatory. Black pepper increases bioavailability 2000%.

**Bone broth:**
Collagen + glutamine for gut lining repair. Gut-brain axis: inflamed gut → systemic inflammation → neuroinflammation.

**Fermented foods (sauerkraut, kimchi):**
Probiotic support for gut microbiome restoration. Critical after substance use history.

**Dark chocolate (85%+):**
Flavanols boost cerebral blood flow. Contains caffeine + theobromine — daytime only due to stimulant effect.
