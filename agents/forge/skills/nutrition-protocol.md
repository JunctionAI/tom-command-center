## CALORIE & MACRO TARGETS BY PHASE

**Phase 1 (Foundation): Maintenance + brain fuel (~2500 kcal/day)**
- Protein: 140g (1.8g/kg) — enough for recovery without surplus
- Carbs: 250g (complex carbs: sweet potato, rice, oats)
- Fats: 90g (avocado, olive oil, fish, nuts)
- Focus: nutrient density for brain repair, not muscle gain yet

**Phase 2 (Brain Recovery): Slight surplus (~2700 kcal/day)**
- Protein: 150g
- Carbs: 280g (fuel for increased exercise)
- Fats: 90g
- Pre-workout meal becomes important: carbs + protein 60-90 min before gym

**Phase 3-5 (Transformation+): Surplus for growth (~2800-3200 kcal/day)**
- Protein: 156g minimum (2g/kg)
- Carbs: 300-350g (increase around training)
- Fats: 85-95g
- Tracking becomes important: use MyFitnessPal or similar for 2-4 weeks to calibrate

## GUT HEALING PROTOCOL

Tyler's gut issues (bloating, left-sided pain, constant hunger):

**Week 1-2: Identify triggers**
- Food diary: log everything eaten + gut symptoms 1-10 after each meal
- Common triggers to test: dairy, gluten, high-FODMAP foods, caffeine
- The left-sided pain: track when it occurs (before/after eating, specific foods, stress-related?)

**Week 3-4: Elimination trial**
- Remove top 2 suspected triggers for 2 weeks
- If symptoms improve: reintroduce one at a time, 3 days apart

**Ongoing gut repair:**
- Bone broth daily (collagen + glutamine for gut lining)
- Fermented foods with every meal (sauerkraut, kimchi, kefir)
- L-glutamine 5g daily
- Probiotics daily
- Chew food thoroughly (20+ chews per bite — seriously)
- Don't drink large amounts of water with meals (dilutes stomach acid)
- Cooked vegetables over raw (easier to digest while gut heals)

**If left-sided pain persists beyond 4 weeks of protocol:** Recommend Dr. Robin Kelly investigation. Could be adhesions from childhood hernia, and worth ruling out anything structural.
