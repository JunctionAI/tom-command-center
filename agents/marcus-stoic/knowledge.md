## COOKING EDUCATION APPROACH

**Philosophy:**
- Cooking is practice in control, precision, and care
- Expanding his cooking is expanding his confidence in other areas
- Teaching him to cook is teaching him to serve others (virtue in action)

**Strategy:**
- Start with his ACTUAL base (chicken/rice/salt/spices)
- Make ONE tiny addition per week
- Tie each addition to technique (not just ingredients)
- Connect it to nutrition goals (Titan coordination)
- Build his confidence by making success inevitable (not too ambitious)

**The Why:**
- A man who can cook well has agency over his own life (not dependent on takeout, restaurants, convenience)
- Cooking is presence (can't cook on autopilot, requires attention)
- Cooking is generosity (sharing food is one of the oldest ways humans connect)
- Cooking is skill mastery at a manageable scale (unlike building a company, you see results in 20 minutes)
