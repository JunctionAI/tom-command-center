### DANIEL AMEN FRAMEWORK

**Core Principles:**
1. **Brain Blood Flow** — The foundation. Better blood flow = better function. Increased with cardio, meditation, certain supplements
2. **Brain Region Function** — Different regions = different functions (prefrontal cortex = impulse control, temporal lobes = memory, etc.)
3. **Avoid Brain Toxins** — Alcohol, drugs, poor sleep, chronic stress, physical trauma all damage blood flow and function
4. **Optimize Brain-Protective Factors** — Sleep, exercise, nutrition, meditation, learning, social connection
5. **Neuroplasticity** — Brain can rewire itself. Recovery is possible at any age, especially at 25

**Amen's Brain Types (for context):**
- Balanced brain (ideal)
- Overfocused brain (gets stuck on thoughts — sounds like Tom)
- ADD/Hyperactive brain (scattered, impulsive)
- Impulsive brain (risk-taking, poor judgment)
- Sad/anxious brain (mood instability)
- Temporal lobe brain (memory, emotional regulation issues)

Tom likely shows **overfocused traits** (extreme obsessions, context-switching difficulty) + possible **temporal lobe involvement** (memory, social comprehension issues). Recovery will address both.

### INTERVENTION TOOLKIT

**Tier 1: Immediate, evidence-based, sustainable:**
- Cardiovascular exercise (30+ min daily) — proven to increase brain blood flow and BDNF
- Sleep optimization (8+ hours, consistent schedule) — critical for brain repair and memory consolidation
- Meditation/mindfulness (10-20 min daily) — increases prefrontal cortex blood flow, emotional regulation
- Omega-3 supplementation (2-4g daily) — protects brain health, reduces inflammation
- Substance cessation (CRITICAL) — no alcohol, no drugs, minimal weed. Brain cannot heal if being assaulted daily

**Tier 2: Advanced cognitive training:**
- Spaced repetition learning (build knowledge systematically)
- Working memory exercises (n-back training, dual-task protocols)
- Social engagement protocols (deliberate social practice, conversation structure)
- Cold exposure (brief cold showers: 30-60 sec) — activates brain resilience, increases BDNF

**Tier 3: Tracking & measurement:**
- Sleep tracking (duration, deep sleep %, REM %)
- Mood tracking (1-10 daily, note energy, anxiety, focus quality)
- Cognitive performance (memory tests, focus duration, comprehension)
- Social tracking (conversations initiated, quality of presence, ease)
