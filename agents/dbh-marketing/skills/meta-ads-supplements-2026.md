# Meta Ads for Supplements 2026

## The Andromeda Reality

Meta's Andromeda algorithm (late 2024) changed everything. **Creative is now the primary targeting signal.** The algorithm analyzes your visuals, copy, and engagement patterns to find audiences—not the other way around.

**What this means:**
- Broad targeting + diverse creative > narrow targeting + single creative
- Visual variety is mandatory (same image + different text = penalized)
- Creative lifespan is ~21 days before fatigue
- Organic-style content outperforms polished ads by 40%

## Quick Start Workflow

```
1. BRIEF → Define product, audience, objective
2. HOOKS → Write 3-5 hook variations (see Hook Formulas)
3. CREATIVE → Generate diverse visuals (4+ styles)
4. COPY → Write primary text, headlines, CTAs
5. COMPLIANCE → Check against health policy
6. STRUCTURE → Set up Advantage+ or manual campaign
7. LAUNCH → Deploy with proper tracking
8. ITERATE → Refresh creative every 2-3 weeks
```

## Core Principles (2026)

### 1. Creative Diversity is Non-Negotiable

Meta's visual recognition penalizes repetitive imagery. Every campaign needs:
- 3+ distinct visual styles (product, lifestyle, UGC)
- 3+ hook variations per concept
- Mix of formats (static, video, carousel)

### 2. Organic Beats Polished

Top performers in 2025-2026:
- Selfie-style videos (+40% vs studio)
- TikTok-native aesthetics
- Screen recordings and demos
- "Caught on camera" moments

### 3. Video First, Mobile First

- 60%+ of Meta time is video consumption
- Hook must land in 0-3 seconds
- 4:5 vertical outperforms 1:1 by 15%
- 80% watch sound-off → captions mandatory

### 4. Advantage+ is Default

ASC (Advantage+ Sales Campaigns) delivers:
- 22% higher revenue per dollar
- 9% lower CPA on average
- Better with broad targeting + creative variety

### 5. 21-Day Creative Refresh Cycle

- Days 1-7: Learning phase
- Days 8-14: Peak performance
- Days 15-21: Declining efficiency
- Day 21+: Creative fatigue, refresh needed

## Hook Formulas That Convert

### The 3-Second Rule
Your hook must deliver impact in 3 seconds or less. After this threshold, performance drops dramatically.

### Top Hook Frameworks

**1. Pattern Interrupt**
```
"Stop scrolling if you [specific pain point]"
"Wait—this changes everything about [category]"
```

**2. Curiosity Gap**
```
"I was skeptical until I saw what happened after week 3"
"The ingredient Big Pharma doesn't want you to know about"
```

**3. Social Proof Lead**
```
"80% of users saw results in 8 weeks"
"Join 10,000+ Kiwis who've discovered..."
```

**4. Transformation Hook**
```
"From struggling to climb stairs to hiking again"
"I went from [before state] to [after state]"
```

**5. Question Hook**
```
"Still taking joint supplements that don't work?"
"What if your morning stiffness didn't have to be permanent?"
```

**6. Mythbusting**
```
"Everything you've been told about joint health is wrong"
"Forget fish oil—here's what actually works"
```

### Video Hook Techniques

| Technique | How It Works |
|-----------|--------------|
| Post-It Reveal | Question on post-it covering product, peel to reveal |
| Blurred Focus | Text/product blurred, slowly comes into focus |
| Multi-Creator Mashup | Rapid cuts of different people with product |
| Split Screen | Before/after or us vs them comparison |
| POV Scroll | First-person phone scrolling to your ad |

## Campaign Structure 2026

### Recommended Setup

```
ACCOUNT STRUCTURE
├── Advantage+ Sales Campaign (70% budget)
│   ├── Broad targeting (country-level)
│   ├── 6-10 diverse creatives
│   └── 7-day click attribution
│
├── Manual Campaign - Retargeting (20% budget)
│   ├── Website visitors 7-30 days
│   ├── Video viewers 50%+
│   └── Cart abandoners
│
└── Manual Campaign - Testing (10% budget)
    ├── New hooks and angles
    ├── 3-5 day tests
    └── Graduate winners to ASC
```

### Budget Guidelines

| Daily Budget | Recommendation |
|--------------|----------------|
| <$50/day | Single ASC, 4-6 creatives |
| $50-200/day | ASC + Retargeting |
| $200-500/day | Full structure above |
| $500+/day | Add prospecting manual campaigns |

### Bidding Strategy

- **Default:** Lowest cost (let Meta optimize)
- **Scaling:** Cost cap at 1.2x target CPA
- **Mature accounts:** ROAS target bidding

## Compliance Quick Reference

### Approved Language
- Supports, promotes, helps, maintains
- May assist, contributes to
- Natural support, wellness support

### Prohibited Language
- Cures, treats, heals, fixes
- Prevents, eliminates, stops
- Guaranteed, proven (without evidence)
- Miracle, breakthrough, instant

### High-Risk (Extra Scrutiny)
- Weight loss
- Pain relief
- Mental health
- Anti-aging
- Disease-specific claims

### Required for Health Supplements
- Age targeting 18+
- No before/after implying transformation
- No personal attributes ("you have joint pain")
- Disclaimer for statistical claims

## Metrics & Optimization

### Primary KPIs
| Metric | Target | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| ROAS | 3-5x | Blended, not last-click |
| CAC | <$30 | Varies by AOV |
| CTR | >1.5% | Hook effectiveness |
| Hook Rate | >25% | 3-sec video views |
| MER | >3x | Total revenue / total ad spend |

### When to Kill Creative
- CTR <0.8% after 1000 impressions
- CPA >2x target after $50 spend
- Frequency >3 in 7 days

### When to Scale Creative
- ROAS >4x sustained 3+ days
- CTR >2% with volume
- Stable CPA at increased budget
