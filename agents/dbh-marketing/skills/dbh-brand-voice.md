# Deep Blue Health Brand Voice & Copywriting Guide

## Brand Foundation

### Who is Deep Blue Health?
Deep Blue Health is an innovative, premium natural health supplement producer established in 2004. Based in New Zealand, they are exclusively NZ owned and operated, working with distributors in 15+ countries worldwide. They partner with New Zealand Health Manufacturing, an award-winning dietary supplement manufacturer, ensuring all products are produced in GMP-registered facilities.

### Brand Positioning: "NATURE'S SECRETS"
New Zealand is home to many of the most potent and pure natural ingredients available for human health. Deep Blue Health products are formulated with premium ingredients traceable back to the hive, sea, mountains, and fields of their origin.

### Core Brand Values: PURE | POTENT | TRACEABLE
- **PURE**: No artificial flavourings, colourings, or preservatives. Clean formulations.
- **POTENT**: High-strength, concentrated extracts. Premium-grade ingredients.
- **TRACEABLE**: Ingredients sourced from known NZ locations. Transparent supply chain.

## Tone of Voice

### Primary Voice Characteristics

**1. Educational-First, Sales-Second**
Position the brand as a trusted health advisor, not an aggressive marketer. Lead with education.

**2. Scientific Authority + Conversational Accessibility**
Balance technical credibility with friendly, approachable language. Use "you" language.

**3. Warm & Trustworthy**
Build trust through transparency about sourcing, processes, and quality standards.

**4. Proudly New Zealand**
Emphasize NZ heritage naturally. Let quality speak for itself.

### Voice Examples

**Good - Conversational + Scientific:**
> "We get asked this question all the time: When's the best time to take my Green Lipped Mussel capsules?"

**Good - Surprising fact hook:**
> "Here's a Fun Fact: You're actually taller in the morning. It sounds strange, but it's true."

**Good - Honest positioning:**
> "The truth is there's no magic pill but you can take supplements to maximize the little exercise you can do!"

**Good - Body mechanism explanation:**
> "During the deeper stages of sleep, your body releases growth hormone - the same hormone that helps repair muscles, tendons, cartilage, and connective tissue."

**Avoid - Aggressive sales:**
> ~~"Buy now before it's too late! This miracle supplement will change your life!"~~

**Avoid - Vague claims:**
> ~~"Feel amazing with our powerful wellness formula!"~~

## Copywriting Formulas

### Product Description Structure
1. **Headline**: Benefit-focused, 5-8 words
2. **Tagline**: Single line capturing key benefit
3. **Opening**: What the product is + key benefit
4. **The Secret/Why It Works**: Science/mechanism explanation
5. **Benefits List**: 3-5 specific benefits with explanation
6. **Quality Guarantee**: NZ sourcing + manufacturing standards
7. **Ingredients**: Precise amounts + "No artificial..." statement
8. **Dosage**: Clear instructions
9. **Cautions**: Safety information
10. **FAQs**: 3-5 common questions answered

### Blog Article Structure
1. **Hook**: Problem-based or curiosity-driven question
2. **Problem**: Define reader pain points with empathy
3. **Science**: Explain body mechanisms and ingredient science
4. **Solution**: Introduce product as natural answer
5. **Practical**: How to use, when to expect results
6. **CTA**: Soft, benefit-focused call to action

### Headline Formulas
- **How-To/Benefit**: "Why Taking Your Green Lipped Mussel at Night Could Make All the Difference"
- **Problem/Solution**: "Breaking Bad Cycles with an Age-Old Supplement"
- **Authority/Education**: "Science Behind SeaMAX"
- **Lifestyle Aspirational**: "Power of Pure Hemp Protein: Your Path to Wellness"
- **Comprehensive Guide**: "Green-Lipped Mussel + Omega-3 for Dog Joints: Evidence & Guide"

## Standard Phrases & Messaging

### Quality & Trust (Use in every product)
- "NZ laboratory quality tested"
- "Made under internationally recognised HACCP Principles and Good Manufacturing Practices"
- "No artificial flavours, colours or preservatives"
- "Sourced from the pristine waters/farms of New Zealand"
- "100% New Zealand owned and operated"

### Dosage Language
- "Take one or two capsules daily as a dietary food supplement or as professionally advised"
- "Store below 30°c in a dry place"

### Results Timeline
- "Most people notice improvements within 2-4 weeks"
- "For serious health conditions, give it at least 4-8 weeks of consistent use"
- "Results vary from person to person"

### Cautions Template
- "If on prescription medication, check with your doctor first"
- "[Product-specific caution, e.g., 'Not suitable for those with allergies to bee products']"

## Quick Checklist

Before publishing DBH content, verify:
- [ ] Includes "Pure | Potent | Traceable" messaging where appropriate
- [ ] Mentions New Zealand sourcing/quality
- [ ] Uses educational tone (not aggressive sales)
- [ ] Includes specific ingredients with amounts
- [ ] Has "No artificial flavours, colours or preservatives" statement
- [ ] Provides realistic timelines (4-8 weeks typical)
- [ ] Includes quality guarantee language (HACCP, GMP)
- [ ] Contains appropriate cautions/disclaimers
- [ ] Uses second person ("you/your") for connection
- [ ] Balances scientific credibility with accessibility
