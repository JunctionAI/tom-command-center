# Deep Blue Health Meta Ads

## Quick Start

1. **Identify the product** - Check [product-library.md](references/product-library.md) for benefits, claims, pricing
2. **Choose ad type** - See Ad Frameworks below
3. **Generate copy** - Use formulas from [copywriting.md](references/copywriting.md)
4. **Create image prompts** - Use templates from [image-prompts.md](references/image-prompts.md)
5. **Review compliance** - Check claims against [compliance.md](references/compliance.md)

## Brand Voice

**Tone:** Premium, trustworthy, natural, scientifically-grounded, warmly confident

**Positioning:** New Zealand's premium marine wellness brand. 20+ years of expertise. Pure ingredients from pristine waters.

**Key differentiators:**
- NZ origin and purity (Marlborough Sounds, pristine waters)
- GMP certified, lab tested every batch
- No fillers, no artificial additives
- Family-owned, 20+ years in business

**Voice examples:**
- YES: "Nature's most powerful joint support, from New Zealand's pristine waters"

- NO: "Guaranteed to fix your joints forever"

## Ad Frameworks

### Framework 1: Problem-Agitate-Solution (PAS)
Best for: Cold audiences, awareness campaigns

```
HOOK: [Pain point question or statement]
AGITATE: [Expand on the problem's impact]
SOLUTION: [Product as the answer]
CTA: [Clear action]
```

### Framework 2: Social Proof Lead
Best for: Warm audiences, retargeting

```
HOOK: [Statistic or testimonial]
BRIDGE: [Why this matters]
PRODUCT: [Brief feature/benefit]
CTA: [Clear action]
```

### Framework 3: Origin Story
Best for: Brand building, premium positioning

```
HOOK: [NZ origin angle]
STORY: [Sourcing/quality narrative]
BENEFIT: [What this means for customer]
CTA: [Clear action]
```

### Framework 4: Comparison
Best for: Differentiation, quality-conscious buyers

```
HOOK: [Quality question]
CONTRAST: [Us vs typical alternatives]
PROOF: [Certification/testing]
CTA: [Clear action]
```

## Image Generation Workflow

1. **Select style** from [image-prompts.md](references/image-prompts.md):
   - Product Hero (clean product shots)
   - Lifestyle (people in motion, active living)
   - Origin (NZ landscapes, ocean, mussels)
   - Trust (hand holding product, badges)

2. **Choose tool and format:**
   - Krea: Best for product photography, style transfer
   - Midjourney: Best for lifestyle, aspirational imagery
   - DALL-E: Best for conceptual, comparison layouts
   - Ideogram: Best for text integration

3. **Generate 4 variations**, select best, iterate

## A/B Testing Framework

**Always test:**
- 2-3 headline variations
- 2 image styles (product vs lifestyle)
- 2 CTA variations

**Test priority order:**
1. Hook/headline (biggest impact)
2. Image (second biggest)
3. Body copy
4. CTA text

**Minimum test duration:** 3-5 days or 1000 impressions per variant

## Output Specifications

### Meta Ad Specs
- **Square (Feed):** 1080x1080px

- **Landscape:** 1200x628px

- **Headline:** 27 chars (visible), 255 max
- **Description:** 27 chars (visible)
