## STRATEGIC RATIONALE

### Why Anti-Aging / Marine Collagen NOW

1. **Seasonal timing:** March = end of NZ summer. Sun damage concerns peak. Skin recovery messaging resonates.
2. **Market demand:** Marine collagen is the fastest-growing supplement category globally (CAGR 8.3%). NZ consumers searching "marine collagen benefits" at 400-900/month.
3. **Campaign sequence logic:** Pure Pets (Campaign #1) was audited and completed. Anti-Aging is the bridge to GLM (our flagship) — it's a different audience segment that hasn't been activated recently.
4. **Competitive gap:** No NZ marine collagen brand dominates the SEO/content space. DBH can own "NZ marine collagen" positioning.
5. **Product-market fit:** Marine Collagen is DBH's second-strongest product by margin. Untapped in recent campaigns.
