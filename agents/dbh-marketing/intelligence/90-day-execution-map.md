1. **A campaign is significantly outperforming** (>2x target) → Extend by 3-5 days, increase Meta budget
2. **A campaign is significantly underperforming** (<50% target) → Cut short at Day 10, redirect budget to SEO/next campaign
3. **A test produces a breakthrough** (>30% improvement) → Immediately apply to current campaign
4. **External event creates opportunity** (competitor stumble, seasonal moment, viral content) → Insert flash campaign between planned campaigns
5. **Capacity constraint** (Tom overloaded, Roie unavailable) → Simplify: reduce Meta ads to 2, reduce emails to 3, maintain SEO output

### What never changes:
- SEO articles continue regardless (AI-produced, minimal human time)
- Testing cadence continues (always 2+ tests per cycle)
- Weekly intelligence review happens every Friday
- Playbooks update monthly
- Decision documentation on every campaign
