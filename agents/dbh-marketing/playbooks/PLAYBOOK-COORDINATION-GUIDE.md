## THE THREE PLAYBOOKS

### 1. EMAIL PLAYBOOK
**File:** `email-playbook.md`
**Purpose:** "What messaging works in email for each segment?"
**Contains:**
- 7 proven winners (Exclusive Access, Event Launch, Testimonial, etc.)
- 5 email formulas with segment-specific versions
- Segment targeting rules (frequency, exclusions, discount strategy)
- KPI benchmarks by segment
- Integration with flows

**When to use:** Planning any email campaign or flow

---

### 2. CREATIVE PLAYBOOK
**File:** `creative-playbook.md`
**Purpose:** "What creative strategy works across all channels?"
**Contains:**
- 3 creative formulas (TRUST + SOCIAL PROOF, EXCLUSIVITY + SCARCITY, GIFT PSYCHOLOGY)
- Cross-channel proven winners (Meta + Email)
- Creative hierarchy (what to prioritize)
- Meta-specific guidance + Email-specific guidance

**When to use:** Planning campaigns, ads, or creative testing

---

### 3. CUSTOMER SEGMENTS PLAYBOOK
**File:** `customer-segments-playbook.md`
**Purpose:** "Who are our customers and what lifecycle stage are they in?"
**Contains:**
- 6 lifecycle segments (New → First-Time → Repeat → Loyal → At-Risk → Churned)
- 4 engagement tiers (High → Moderate → Low → Never)
- Email strategy per segment
- Flow recommendations
- Product-affinity segments (Joint Health, Hair, Pet, Immunity)
- Campaign targeting rules by segment

**When to use:** Defining your audience, planning segment strategy

## HOW THEY WORK TOGETHER

### The Three-Layer Decision Model

```
LAYER 1: SEGMENT
"Who am I targeting?"
↓ (Check: Customer Segments Playbook)
├─ New Subscribers (10-15%)
├─ First-Time Buyers (15-20%)
├─ Repeat Buyers (20-25%)
├─ Loyal Customers (25-30%)
├─ At-Risk (5-10%)
└─ Churned (5-10%, SUPPRESS)

LAYER 2: GOAL / CREATIVE FORMULA
"What's my objective? What creative approach?"
↓ (Check: Creative Playbook + Email Playbook)
├─ If goal = Drive trust → Use TRUST + SOCIAL PROOF formula
├─ If goal = Increase urgency → Use EXCLUSIVITY + SCARCITY formula
├─ If goal = Overcome objection → Use GIFT PSYCHOLOGY formula
└─ Pick email formula that matches (Testimonial, Benefit-specific, etc.)

LAYER 3: EXECUTION
"How do I execute this?"
↓ (Check: Email Playbook templates + Creative Playbook specs)
├─ Subject line: Use segment-specific formula (Email Playbook p. 7-8)
├─ Body: Use creative formula positioning (Creative Playbook p. 3-4)
├─ Images: Follow creative specs (Creative Playbook p. 10+)
└─ CTA: Match segment psychology (Email Playbook segment rules)

RESULT: Coordinated campaign with proven messaging + creative + targeting
```

## DECISION TREES

### "I need to plan an email campaign"

```
Start: What's my goal?
│
├─ Drive FIRST purchases?
│  └─ Segment: NEW SUBSCRIBERS
│     └─ Creative: Educational + Social Proof (Creative, Winner 1)
│        └─ Email Formula: Benefit-Specific or Testimonial (Email p. 7)
│           └─ Template: "Discover [Benefit]" subject (Email p. 7)
│              └─ Frequency: 3-4x/week (Email Segment Rules)
│                 └─ Expected: 40%+ open, $1K-3K revenue
│
├─ Drive REPEAT purchases?
│  └─ Segment: FIRST-TIME BUYERS
│     └─ Creative: Social Proof + Value (Creative, Winner 1)
│        └─ Email Formula: Testimonial + Benefit (Email p. 6)
│           └─ Template: "Why customers buy again" subject (Email p. 6)
│              └─ Frequency: 2-3x/week (6 weeks) (Email Segment Rules)
│                 └─ Expected: 35%+ open, $2K-5K revenue
│
├─ Increase AOV?
│  └─ Segment: REPEAT BUYERS
│     └─ Creative: Exclusivity + Scarcity (Creative, Winner 2)
│        └─ Email Formula: Exclusive/Early Access or Testimonial (Email p. 6)
│           └─ Template: "Exclusive [Bundle]" subject (Email p. 6)
│              └─ Frequency: 2-3x/week (Email Segment Rules)
│                 └─ Expected: 35%+ open, $4K-8K revenue
│
├─ Maximize LTV?
│  └─ Segment: LOYAL CUSTOMERS
│     └─ Creative: Exclusivity + Scarcity (Creative, Winner 2)
│        └─ Email Formula: VIP Exclusive (Email p. 6)
│           └─ Template: "VIP: 48-Hour Early Access" subject (Email p. 6)
│              └─ Frequency: 2-3x/week (Email Segment Rules)
│                 └─ Expected: 50%+ open, $8K-12K revenue
│
└─ Win back lapsed?
   └─ Segment: AT-RISK
      └─ Creative: Scarcity + Loss Aversion (Creative, Winner 2)
         └─ Email Formula: Last Chance (Email p. 8)
            └─ Template: "Final Hours: [Offer] Ends Tonight" (Email p. 8)
               └─ Frequency: 2-3x/week (2 weeks intensive) (Email Segment Rules)
                  └─ Expected: 25%+ open, $1K-3K revenue
```

## MONTHLY PLAYBOOK REVIEW PROCESS

**At end of each month, follow this process:**

1. **Gather data** (30 min)
   - Pull campaign performance by segment
   - Check actual KPIs vs. playbook targets
   - Note any surprises (over/under-performing segments)

2. **Compare to playbooks** (30 min)
   - Open Email Playbook segment KPI targets
   - Open Creative Playbook proven winners section
   - Open Segments Playbook strategy section
   - Did results match predictions?

3. **Update playbooks** (1 hour)
   - If segment performed better than expected: Note in CHANGELOG why
   - If formula underperformed: Test different formula next month
   - If new pattern emerged: Add to EMERGING PATTERNS section
   - Update KPI targets if needed (but only with 3+ data points)

4. **Plan next month** (30 min)
   - Pick top 3 performing segment × formula combinations
   - Plan to repeat them (with fresh creative, new angle)
   - Pick 1 test (different formula or segment)
   - Update CHANGELOG with decisions

5. **Communicate** (15 min)
   - Share findings with team
   - Update CLAUDE.md if major discoveries
   - Note any new team guidelines emerging from data

## PLAYBOOK HIERARCHY (What to Trust)

When information conflicts between playbooks, follow this priority:

1. **Email Playbook** (highest) — Specific to DBH email performance, 133 campaigns analyzed
2. **Creative Playbook** — Cross-channel proven (Meta + Email), validated multiple times
3. **Segments Playbook** — Defines audiences, supported by Klaviyo data + analysis
4. **General knowledge** (lowest) — Only use if playbooks don't cover it

Example: If Creative Playbook says "discounts drive ROAS" but Email Playbook says "gift framing beats discounts", follow Email Playbook for email campaigns (but Creative might be right for Meta ads).

## COMMON COORDINATION SCENARIOS

### Scenario 1: "I want to launch a new product"
1. **Segments Playbook:** Target Repeat Buyers + Loyal Customers (most likely to try new products)
2. **Creative Playbook:** Use TRUST + SOCIAL PROOF formula (7.78x ROAS, highest sustained)
3. **Email Playbook:** Use Event Launch formula (p. 7) or Exclusive Access (p. 6)
4. **Execution:** Meta ads with customer testimonial + Email with early access offer to engaged segments

### Scenario 2: "Open rates are declining"
1. **Email Playbook:** Check segment-specific open rate targets (should vary 25%-50%)
2. **Segments Playbook:** Are you sending to the right segments? (Loyal should be 50%+, broad should be 22%)
3. **Creative Playbook:** Test new angle (is it still TRUST based or has novelty worn off?)
4. **Action:** Segment your next campaign, use different creative formula

### Scenario 3: "We need to grow revenue 25% this quarter"
1. **Segments Playbook:** 80% of revenue from Loyal (25-30%) + Repeat (20-25%) buyers
2. **Email Playbook:** These segments expect 50%+ and 35%+ open rates respectively
3. **Creative Playbook:** Use EXCLUSIVITY + SCARCITY formula (8.45x ROAS peak performance)
4. **Plan:** 3x/week to Loyal with exclusive offers + 2-3x/week to Repeat with bundles = $18K-24K/month email revenue

### Scenario 4: "I'm building the first purchase flow"
1. **Segments Playbook:** First-Time Buyers are critical (15-20% of list, gateway to loyalty)
2. **Email Playbook:** Use Testimonial + Benefit formula (Formula 1, p. 6)
3. **Creative Playbook:** Lead with customer story showing "2nd purchase motivator" angle
4. **Execution:** 5-email sequence over 50 days, each using slightly different testimonial/benefit combo
