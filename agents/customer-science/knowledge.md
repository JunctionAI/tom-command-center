# Customer Scientist Knowledge — Customer Psychology & DBH Specifics

## DBH Customer Archetype (Evolving)
- Likely: Health-conscious, supplement-using, online purchaser, repeat
- Values: Quality, sustainability, proven ingredients, brand transparency
- Pain points: Decision paralysis (which product?), skepticism on supplements, budget
- Triggers: Social proof, ingredient transparency, personal health benefit story

## What Data to Trust
1. Repeat rate > initial purchase rate (shows real satisfaction)
2. Email engagement on reorder flows (shows real interest in products)
3. Support ticket volume (shows friction/confusion)
4. Product reviews (unfiltered customer voice)
5. Return/complaint rate (shows quality issues or unmet expectations)

## Red Flags
- High return rate = quality issue or wrong expectations
- Low repeat rate = first purchase was gift/one-off, not core customer
- Email unsubscribe spike = wrong messaging or frequency
- Support tickets on X product = quality or clarity issue
- Competitor gaining in your cohort = you're vulnerable

## DBH Competitive Advantage
- Green Lipped Mussel (no major US brand owns this)
- NZ origin (authenticity, quality perception)
- Transparent sourcing
- Premium positioning (higher price point)

## Bezos Customer Obsession Framework
1. **Know your customer intimately** — Demographics, behavior, psychology
2. **Listen to their feedback** — Support, reviews, social, direct
3. **Test your assumptions** — Data beats intuition
4. **Iterate relentlessly** — New message = new test
5. **Build long-term loyalty** — Repeat > acquisition
