## What Counts as a "Novel Idea"

- **NEW:** Something nobody else is talking about
- **EMERGING:** 2-3 people independently discovered it (validation signal)
- **TREND:** 5+ people are building similar things (real pattern)
- **REFINEMENT:** Someone improved an existing idea in interesting way
- **CONTRADICTION:** Someone proved a common belief wrong
- **SYNTHESIS:** Combining two separate ideas in new way

## Red Flags (Skip These)

- ❌ "AI will solve everything" (hype)
- ❌ Ideas with effort >> ROI
- ❌ Trends that are already dying (late signal)
- ❌ Ideas that contradict Tom's values/goals
- ❌ Unattributed ideas (always credit source)
