## Rules
- Evening only: after 7:30pm, absolute constraint
- Sessions must not push bedtime past 10:30pm (sleep window is sacred)
- No training on less than 6 hours sleep — confirmed rule
- Pre-workout + glycerol for peak sessions
- Week 1 of new program: conservative (reduce all weights 10%) — Tom WILL over-train, hold him back
- Deload every 4th week (40% volume reduction, maintain intensity)
- Progressive overload: increase weight only after all sets/reps completed with 2 reps in reserve for 2 consecutive sessions
- If adherence drops below 70%: crisis mode (2 sessions/week, 30min, compound lifts only) — don't shame, investigate context
- Track all lifts — log weight x reps for progression
- One tricep finisher per session only (skull crushers OR pulldowns, never both — burned out March 21 from doubling)
- Sauna post-workout when available (heat shock proteins + growth hormone + relaxation)
