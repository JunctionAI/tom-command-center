## Daily Targets (Phase 2: 75kg, brain repair + muscle building)

- Calories: 2,800-3,000
- Protein: 150-165g (2-2.2g/kg)
- Fats: 80-100g (prioritise omega-3 and monounsaturated)
- Carbs: 300-350g (brain fuel — don't cut carbs for overfocused brain type)

## 5-Meal Rotation (Simplicity = Adherence)

### Meal 1: Brain Breakfast (8:30 AM)
- 3-4 eggs scrambled/fried in olive oil
- 2 slices sourdough toast
- Half avocado
- Handful spinach (sauteed or fresh)
- Coffee (no more after 2pm)
- ~650 cal, ~35g protein

### Meal 2: Power Lunch (1:00 PM)
- 200g chicken breast (grilled/pan)
- 1 cup cooked rice
- Broccoli + mixed greens with olive oil
- ~550 cal, ~45g protein

### Meal 3: Brain Smoothie (3:30 PM)
- 1 scoop protein powder (30g protein)
- 1 banana
- 1 cup blueberries (frozen works)
- Handful spinach
- 1 tbsp almond butter
- 250ml milk
- ~500 cal, ~35g protein
- Blueberry anthocyanins cross the blood-brain barrier — this is a brain repair shake

### Meal 4: Pre-Training Snack (6:30 PM)
- Apple + 2 tbsp peanut butter
- OR Greek yoghurt + walnuts + honey
- ~300 cal, ~15g protein

### Meal 5: Performance Dinner (post-training, ~9:30 PM)
**Option A — Fish Night (2-3x/week for omega-3):**
- 200g salmon fillet
- Sweet potato (medium)
- Mixed salad with olive oil + lemon
- ~650 cal, ~40g protein

**Option B — Meat Night:**
- 200g lean beef or chicken
- 1 cup cooked rice
- Roasted vegetables (capsicum, courgette, onion, garlic)
- Drizzle olive oil
- ~600 cal, ~45g protein

### Daily total: ~2,750-3,050 cal, ~160g protein
