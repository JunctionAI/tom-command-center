# Tom Hall-Taylor -- Full Background

## The Short Version
25-year-old New Zealander (born England). Serial entrepreneur since 14.
Bitcoin trader at 16 (turned hundreds into $70K, then lost most of it).
Travelled and hustled through London, Vietnam, Bali, India, Hong Kong.
Now CMO at Deep Blue Health (DTC supplements, NZ), running Junction AI
(marketing agency), and building towards healthcare/tech long-term.

Solo operator who leverages AI harder than anyone around him.
Hates meetings, hates corporate, hates being told what to do.
Loves learning, building, and connecting dots across domains.

## Life Timeline

### 0-9 (England)
- Grew up in Northamptonshire. Public school then private.
- Parents described him as having "a very strong will and stubbornness."
- Hated being told what to do. School system muted this.
- Slightly above average academically. Loved sport.
- Normal childhood -- nothing special on paper.

### 9-11 (Auckland, NZ)
- Family moved to Auckland. Tom became the popular novelty kid from the UK.
- UK schooling was harder, so he appeared smarter comparatively.
- Popular, sporty, confident. Life was good.

### 11-13 (Belmont Intermediate)
- Hated it. Shit school, shit education, puberty. No fond memories.

### 14-16 (Westlake Boys High School)
- Competitive, ranked, all-boys environment. Pure competition.
- Toxic aggressive culture that shaped his psyche.
- FIRST BUSINESS: Selling FIFA coins. Bought online with mum's debit card,
  sold at school for cash profit. Started his own coin site, sponsored YouTubers.
  Made low thousands. Tasted business for the first time.
- Prior to this: multiple YouTube channels (pranks, fails, scooter edits,
  trampolining, FIFA). Content creation was natural.

### 16-17 (Bitcoin Era)
- Tennis teammate Fletcher Ollie mentioned Bitcoin (~$300 USD at the time).
- Started trading on LocalBitcoins.com. Accidentally illegal in US (no license).
- Made money via PayPal but put "bitcoin" in references repeatedly -- got his
  AND his mum's PayPal accounts shut. $2.5K frozen for 6 months. Painful wait.
- Old FIFA mate Brad Matthews reconnected. They exchanged info --
  Brad realised they could buy/sell BTC legally in NZ.
- RESULT: Transacted in the low millions of dollars. Profited ~$70K.
- Then lost a large chunk investing in altcoins from Twitter advice.
  "Stupid shit but taught me a lot about money, risk, etc."
- Also competed at National Athletics Championships (Long Jump + Triple Jump).
- Got Excellence Endorsement (top grade). Monster year.

### 17-18 (The Crash)
- Crypto markets dropped. Bank account, emotions, grades, friendships -- everything fell.
- Became depressed. Wanted to drop out of school. Hated the system.
- Arguments with teachers from arrogance and pain.
- Logic: "If I can make money like that outside school, why stay?" Parents forced him.
- In hindsight, glad he finished for closure.

### 18-20 (Post-School Chaos)
- Chose work and travel over university.
- Worked at parents' retail store in Queenstown.
- Travelled Europe with mate Trent.
- Worked in a factory. Planned and executed a music gig (sold out!).
- Went to Victoria Uni for 3 weeks. COVID hit. Dropped out.
- Lockdown at Brad's house -- started intense running + self-improvement.
- Did psychedelics for the first time: "thoroughly changed the way I saw the world,

- Brad and Tom cold-messaged Nick Mowbray (ZURU founder) and Bill Smale (rich listers).

  through the social/networking side. Tom was shy, withdrawn, didn't want involvement.

  as a growth area he needed.

### 24-25 (Agency Growth, Junction AI, Existential Crisis)

  Realised his motivations were about external approval to fill a void.
  "A vision of being ultra successful in order to gain approval externally."

  "A desire to reduce suffering in the world."

  instead of selling products that may not genuinely help.
