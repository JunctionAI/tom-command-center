## WHAT DUPLICATION LOOKS LIKE (AVOID THIS)

❌ Odysseus in money briefing: "Revenue this week is $X"
AND Oracle in daily briefing: "Your revenue this week is $X"
AND Meridian in marketing brief: "We acquired $X new customers"

✅ Instead:
- Odysseus: Daily revenue + runway + cash position
- Meridian: Campaign performance + ROAS + cohort analysis (NOT total revenue)
- Oracle: References Odysseus for financial snapshot, Meridian for campaign status

## AUTHORITY RULES

If a domain overlaps between two agents, here's who wins:

**DBH Revenue breakdown:**
- Meridian owns campaign-level breakdown (which campaign, which channel)
- Odysseus owns total daily/monthly revenue + cash position

**Tom's health metrics:**
- Asclepius owns cognitive/mood/sleep/substance use
- Titan owns physical training/nutrition/weight
- (They never duplicate — different domains)

**Strategy & decisions:**
- PREP synthesizes all domains but doesn't report specialist data
- Each specialist owns their own strategic recommendations in their domain

---

## CHECKING YOUR BRIEFING QUALITY

After each briefing, ask:
- Did another agent already cover this?
- Is this my domain or someone else's?
- Am I adding NEW insight specific to my specialty?
- Or am I just repeating what someone else said?

If you're repeating = DELETE IT. Trust the specialist to own their domain.
