## CONSUMER JOURNEY (What The User Actually Experiences)

### Phase 0: ENTRY
How they find us: content (Tom's brand), referral, word of mouth.
They start talking to the bot. It's a conversation, not a form.

"Hey, I'm [Agent Name]. I'm here to help you actually improve your health — not just tell you what to do, but work with you daily until the results show up in your bloodwork. Tell me what's going on."

Onboarding is CONVERSATIONAL. The bot collects the same data as the questionnaire but through natural dialogue over 2-3 days. No 45-minute intake form. Just talking.

### Phase 1: UNDERSTANDING (Week 1-2)
- Agent learns about them through daily check-ins (3x/day)
- Builds profile conversationally
- Starts with zero-cost wins: sleep hygiene, hydration, basic nutrition
- Agent is already useful before any testing or supplements
- Trust builds through daily consistency and genuine insight

**The natural bridge to testing:**
"Based on what you've told me, I think your energy issues could be related to iron or thyroid function, and your mood patterns suggest we should check vitamin D and B12. But I'm working from what you've shared — to really know, we'd want to see your bloodwork. Want me to sort that for you?"

This is NOT a sales pitch. It's a genuine clinical limitation the agent is being honest about. The science actually requires data.

### Phase 2: BASELINE (Week 2-4)
If user wants blood test:

**Option A (cheapest): Pocket Lab model**
- Agent specifies exactly which tests based on their profile
- Telehealth GP reviews and signs off ($39-59 NZD)
- User goes to nearest community lab (free under public health for eligible NZ residents)
- Total cost to user: $39-59

**Option B (self-pay): BODYiQ / i-screen**
- Agent recommends specific panel
- User orders through partner platform ($250-720 NZD depending on scope)
- Results back in 3-7 days

**Option C (upload existing): Marko model**
- User already has recent blood results from GP
- Takes a photo or uploads PDF
- Agent interprets immediately
- Zero additional cost

Results come back → Agent interprets through clinical frameworks:
"Your Vitamin D is 35 nmol/L — that's in the insufficient range. For brain health and mood, we want you above 100. At your current level, your body can't produce adequate serotonin precursors, which directly affects the mood dips you've been reporting. Here's what we do about it..."

### Phase 3: DIET FIRST (Week 3-5)
Diet before supplements. Always.

"Before we supplement anything, let's see what we can fix with food. Your iron is low — here are three iron-rich meals that fit your existing rotation. Your omega-3 index is 4% — let's add salmon twice a week."

Agent adjusts the existing meal plan based on ACTUAL deficiencies. This is now personalized nutrition, not generic advice.

Track for 2 weeks. See what food alone can move.

### Phase 4: TARGETED SUPPLEMENTATION (Week 5+)
After diet optimization, what's left:

"There are some things that are hard to get enough of through diet alone at your current levels. Your omega-3 index is still at 5% — we need 8%+. That's about 2-4g of EPA+DHA daily. Your Vitamin D is only up to 45 from 35 — food alone won't get you to 100 in NZ winter."

Natural fulfillment ask:
"We can sort your supplement stack — high quality, NZ-made, exactly what your bloodwork says you need. I'll put together your order based on your results. Want me to do that?"

The agent generates an [ORDER:] marker → system creates a curated product bundle from DBH + external where needed → ships to user.

NOT a generic multivitamin. A SPECIFIC stack based on THEIR bloodwork.

### Phase 5: TRACK + CORRELATE (Ongoing)
- Daily check-ins continue (3x/day)
- Subjective metrics + protocol adherence
- Agent correlates improvements with protocol changes
- "Your focus went from 5.2 to 6.8 average over 3 weeks since we started the omega-3 and Vitamin D. That's not coincidence — DHA is literally rebuilding your neuronal membranes."
- This correlation is the PROOF that drives retention and referrals

### Phase 6: RETEST + ADJUST (8-12 weeks)
"It's been 10 weeks since your baseline bloods. Time to retest."

Before/after comparison:
- "Vitamin D: 35 → 85 nmol/L. Massive improvement. On track for target 100."
- "hsCRP (inflammation): 3.2 → 1.1. The omega-3 and diet changes are working."
- "Omega-3 index: 4% → 7.5%. Close to target. Maintain dose."
- "Iron: still low despite diet changes. Let's add a targeted iron supplement."

Protocol adjustment based on NEW data. Supplement stack evolves.

### Phase 7: ADVANCED DIAGNOSTICS (Optional, Premium)
For users who want to go deeper:
- HRV monitoring (wearable integration)
- CGM (continuous glucose monitoring — Levels-style)
- Brain SPECT scan (Amen Clinics — requires US travel, ~$3,000-5,300)
- Gut microbiome testing (Viome/Zoe style)
- Genetic/pharmacogenomic testing (IGENZ in NZ)

These are upsells, not requirements. The core product works without them.

## DIAGNOSTIC LAYERS

Each layer adds confidence to the protocol:

**Layer 1: SUBJECTIVE (what we have now)**
- Self-reported 1-10 scales (focus, mood, energy, social ease)
- Behavioral tracking (substance use, adherence, sleep hours)
- Confidence: Low-medium. Useful for trends but not for causation.

**Layer 2: BEHAVIORAL (tracking data)**
- Training logs (weights, reps, PBs, adherence %)
- Nutrition adherence (meals eaten, protein estimates)
- Protocol adherence (supplements taken, cardio completed)
- Confidence: Medium. Shows what they're actually DOING.

**Layer 3: BIOMARKER (blood panels)**
- Specific nutrient levels, hormones, inflammation markers
- Objective, measurable, before/after comparable
- Confidence: HIGH. This is the science.

**Layer 4: ADVANCED (future)**
- Brain SPECT imaging (blood flow patterns, brain type confirmation)
- HRV (autonomic nervous system health)
- CGM (blood sugar response to specific foods)
- Gut microbiome (personalized nutrition at biological level)
- Confidence: HIGHEST. Multi-dimensional objective data.

Layer 1 alone = educated guessing.
Layer 1 + 2 = pattern detection.
Layer 1 + 2 + 3 = evidence-based personalization.
All four = elite optimization (Lifeforce/longevity clinic tier).

## THE BLOOD PANEL

### Standard Panel (Every User, Baseline + 12-Week Retest)

**Inflammation + Immune:**
- hsCRP (high-sensitivity C-reactive protein) — systemic inflammation marker
- Full Blood Count — immune status, anemia screening

**Nutrients:**
- Vitamin D (25-OH) — target >100 nmol/L (brain, mood, immune, bone)
- Vitamin B12 — target >400 pmol/L (neurotransmitter synthesis)
- Folate — (methylation, neurotransmitter cofactor)
- Iron studies (ferritin, serum iron, transferrin, TIBC) — energy, oxygen transport
- Magnesium (RBC, not serum) — sleep, muscle, 300+ enzymatic reactions
- Zinc — immune, testosterone, neural signalling

**Metabolic:**
- HbA1c — blood sugar stability (cognitive function, energy crashes)
- Fasting glucose — metabolic health baseline
- Full lipid panel (total, HDL, LDL, triglycerides) — cardiovascular + brain health

**Hormones:**
- Total + free testosterone — energy, mood, muscle, motivation
- Cortisol (morning) — HPA axis, stress response
- TSH + free T3/T4 — thyroid (metabolic rate, energy, cognition)

**Liver:**
- ALT, AST, GGT — liver function (relevant for alcohol history, supplement safety)

**Other:**
- Homocysteine — B vitamin status, cardiovascular + brain health risk marker
- Omega-3 index (if available) — target >8%

### Extended Panel (Users with specific conditions)
- DHEA-S (adrenal function)
- Prolactin (dopamine proxy)
- GFAP / UCH-L1 (brain injury biomarkers — for head trauma history)
- Insulin (insulin resistance screening)
- Full thyroid antibodies (autoimmune thyroid)
