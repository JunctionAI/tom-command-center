# Voice Onboarding — Talk to Me for 10-15 Minutes
## Send this to any new user. They record a voice note answering these prompts.

---

Hey! So I've built this AI companion that checks in with you a few times a day, learns about you over time, and helps you actually make progress on your health and fitness goals. It remembers everything, gets smarter about you, and adapts as you go.

To set it up for you, I just need you to talk through these prompts out loud. The easiest way: open ChatGPT voice mode (or any voice-to-text app), talk through the questions below, and it'll transcribe everything for you. Then just send me the transcription over WhatsApp. Way easier than me having to listen through a long voice note.

Don't overthink it — just talk naturally. The more detail the better, but even 10-15 minutes gives us plenty to work with.

---

## THE BASICS

- Your name, age, where you live
- What you do for work / study
- Height and weight if you know it

## WHAT DO YOU WANT?

This is the most important part. Just talk about:

- What does "better health" look like for you? (More energy? Losing weight? Building muscle? Sleeping better? Mental clarity? All of the above?)
- If this thing works perfectly, what's different about your life in 6 months?
- Is there anything specific you've been wanting to do but haven't been able to stick with?

## YOUR BACKGROUND

Give me a bit of your history — this helps the AI understand the full picture:

- Any health issues growing up? (Injuries, allergies, asthma, gut problems, anything)
- Any head injuries or concussions, even minor ones?
- Any psychological stuff — anxiety, depression, ADHD, OCD, anything you've dealt with?
- Any trauma or tough experiences that left a mark? (Only share what you're comfortable with — this stays private, but it helps the AI understand your nervous system and stress patterns)
- Any substance history? (Relevant medically — no judgment)
- Family health history if you know it (parents/grandparents — heart disease, diabetes, mental health, cancer, etc.)

## WHERE ARE YOU AT RIGHT NOW?

Just paint a picture of your current situation:

- How's your energy day to day?
- How's your sleep? (Hours, quality, any issues)
- What does your diet look like? Any foods you avoid or are allergic to?
- Are you exercising? What do you do? What did you used to do?
- How's your stress? What's on your plate?
- Any health conditions, injuries, or medications I should know about?
- Any supplements you're already taking?
- Do you have a GP or any specialists you see?

Don't feel like you need to have problems — this works just as well for someone who's healthy and wants to optimise as it does for someone recovering from something.

## HEALTH DATA

If you have any of these, send them along with your transcript:

- Recent blood test results (photos or PDFs)
- Wearable data (Fitbit, Apple Watch, Oura, Whoop — screenshots of sleep/HRV/heart rate trends)
- Any medical reports, scans, or test results you've had done

Even old results are useful — they give us a baseline to work from.

## HOW DO YOU LIKE TO BE TALKED TO?

- Do you want to be pushed hard and held accountable? Or more gentle support?
- Morning person or night owl?
- What times would work for check-ins? (It'll message you 3x a day — morning, midday, evening)
- Do you prefer short and direct messages or more detailed explanations?

## ANYTHING ELSE?

Anything I haven't asked about that you think matters. Family health history, past experiences with diets or training programs, mental health stuff you're comfortable sharing, whatever.

---

## THAT'S IT

Use ChatGPT voice mode (or any voice-to-text tool) to talk through all of this, then send me the transcription over WhatsApp. Also send any health data (blood tests, wearable screenshots, medical reports) as separate files.

The AI companion will be set up within a day and start checking in with you on Telegram.
