# Health & Recovery Companion — Onboarding Questionnaire
## Universal intake form for building a new AI companion agent

Hey [NAME], Tom here. I've built an AI health companion that checks in with you 3 times a day, learns about you over time, and guides you through an evidence-based recovery plan built from the best clinical research available. It's already running for multiple people and working well.

To set it up properly for you, I need to understand your full picture. Take your time with this. The more detail you give, the better the system works for you. Nothing here is shared with anyone — it goes directly into your private AI companion.

## SECTION 2: YOUR HEALTH HISTORY

**When did your health issues start?**
(Try to identify the earliest signs, even if they seemed minor at the time. Was it a gradual onset or sudden? What was happening in your life at the time?)

**Early health issues (childhood / adolescence):**
(List anything you remember — gut issues, anxiety, injuries, diagnoses, hospital visits, allergies, asthma, anything)

**Head injuries / concussions:**
(Any history? How many, approximate ages, severity. Even "minor" ones count.)

**Drug / substance history:**
(Any psychedelic experiences, bad trips, heavy periods of use? This is medically relevant — substances can rewire the nervous system. Be honest, no judgment.)

**Traumatic experiences:**
(This can be hard to write. Include whatever you're comfortable sharing — childhood, relationships, accidents, loss, bullying, anything that left a mark. The AI companion uses this to understand your nervous system patterns, not to do therapy.)

---

## SECTION 3: CURRENT PRESENTATION

**What are your main symptoms RIGHT NOW?**
(List everything, even things that seem minor or unrelated. Rate each 1-10 for severity if you can.)

For each symptom, note:
- When did it start?
- Is it constant or does it come and go?
- What makes it worse?
- What makes it better?

**Common areas to consider:**
- Fatigue / energy levels
- Sleep (quality, duration, insomnia, nightmares)
- Anxiety (generalised, social, panic attacks, specific triggers)
- Depression / mood
- Pain (where, what type — sharp, dull, burning, aching)
- Headaches / migraines
- Gut issues (IBS, bloating, nausea, appetite)
- Heart rate issues (racing heart, dizziness on standing, POTS symptoms)
- Breathing issues (shortness of breath, hyperawareness of breathing)
- Muscle tension (where — head, neck, shoulders, back, jaw)
- Brain fog / cognitive issues (memory, concentration, word-finding)
- Tinnitus (ringing in ears)
- Visual disturbances (visual snow, floaters, light sensitivity)
- Numbness / tingling / nerve sensations
- Tremors or shaking
- Temperature regulation issues
- Skin issues
- Weight changes (gain or loss, inability to change weight)
- Swallowing / eating difficulties
- OCD tendencies (obsessive thoughts, compulsive behaviours, hyperawareness of body processes)
- Dissociation / depersonalisation (feeling unreal, disconnected from body)

**What is your MAIN concern right now?**
(The thing that distresses you most, the thing you think about most, the thing that limits your life the most)

**What are you most AFRAID of regarding your health?**
(Be specific — what's the fear underneath the symptoms? That it's permanent? That it's getting worse? That something serious is being missed?)

## WHAT HAPPENS NEXT

Once you fill this out:
1. Clinical research: best evidence for your specific conditions
2. Personalised recovery plan: phases tailored to where you are right now
3. AI companion setup: 3 daily check-ins at times you choose
4. The bot learns over time — it never forgets, gets better at supporting you

The bot is NOT a replacement for your doctors or therapists. It's a daily companion that fills the gap between appointments — the 99% of time when you're on your own.
