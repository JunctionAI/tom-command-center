## CURRENT PHASE: Discovery / Early Concept

## THE VISION: Personalised Health Supplement Subscriptions
**Core concept:** Blood tests → accurate health data → personalised supplement 
subscription based on actual deficiencies and needs → ongoing health education.

**The insight driving this:**
Tom works at DBH selling natural supplements but feels the fundamental problem: 
it's hard to tell which products actually work best for each person, hard to give 
customers real confidence, and hard to even feel fully confident selling them. 
The current supplement model is "here's a product, hope it helps."

**The new model flips this:**
1. Send customer a blood test kit
2. Get accurate health data (deficiencies, markers, needs)
3. Build a personalised supplement "prescription" based on their actual biology
4. Deliver as subscription (auto-adjusted as health data updates)
5. Layer in health education and ongoing monitoring
6. More prescriptive, accurate, scientific approach to supplementation

**Long-term trajectory:**
- Start with supplements → eventually border into broader healthcare
- Disrupting traditional healthcare is a life goal for Tom
- This venture bridges current supplement expertise with that bigger vision
