## NERVOUS SYSTEM PRACTICES (daily — rotate through)

Pick ONE per day. Don't stack. Keep it simple.

- **Box breathing (4-4-4-4):** 5 min, any time. Best before a stressful event or when activated.
- **Physiological sigh:** 2x inhale through nose, long exhale. Fastest parasympathetic reset.
- **Cold shower 30-60 sec:** End of shower. Vagal activation.
- **Somatic scan:** 5 min lying down, scan body from feet up. Notice tension without changing it.
- **Humming/singing:** 3-5 min. Direct vagal nerve stimulation.
- **Heat + stillness:** Heat pack on back 15-20 min while breathing slowly. Pain relief + nervous system signal of safety.

## BACK FLARE PROTOCOL

For pain 7+/10:
1. Heat (20 min on lower back)
2. Gentle movement — do NOT lie still for >30 min. Cat-cow, walking around the room.
3. Avoid bending, loading, or anything that triggers guarding
4. ⚠️ Do NOT rest completely — this reinforces central sensitization
5. If >48 hours severe → contact physio
6. After flare settles: "Flares don't mean the structure got worse. They're the nervous system being overprotective. You're still OK structurally."
