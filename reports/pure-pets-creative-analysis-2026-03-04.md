## VIDEO PRODUCTION STRATEGY

### Video Concept (What to Produce)

**Format:** 15-30 second vertical video (TikTok/Reels optimized)

**Story Arc:**
1. **Problem (0-3 sec):** Show dog struggling with movement
   - Slow walk, difficulty jumping, reluctance to play
   - Emotional pain point for pet owners

2. **Transformation (4-15 sec):** Same dog after using Pure Pets
   - Running, jumping, playing with owner
   - Clear visible improvement in mobility

3. **Proof (16-20 sec):** Owner testimonial (10 seconds max)
   - "After 3 weeks, Bella was jumping again"
   - Real owner, real dog, authentic emotion
   - Optional: before/after text overlay

4. **CTA (21-30 sec):**
   - "Shop Pure Pets" with product hero shot
   - "Made in NZ, for your best friend"

**Style:** Documentary/authentic (not glossy/overly produced)
- Natural lighting preferred over studio
- Real homes/outdoor settings
- Genuine owner enthusiasm (not scripted)

## TOOL RECOMMENDATIONS FOR YOUR WORKFLOW

### TIER 1: Video Generation (Choose 1-2)

**Kling** — Best for authentic-looking pet videos
- Strengths: Realistic motion, good for animals, natural colors
- Best for: Before/after transformations
- Cost: ~$15/month
- Timeline: 3-5 min generation per 15-sec video
- **RECOMMENDED** ✓

**Seedance** — Best for lifestyle/testimonial style
- Strengths: Multiple takes quickly, good for variations
- Best for: Owner testimonials, product shots
- Cost: Variable
- Timeline: 2-3 min per video
- Use for: A/B testing variations

**Higgsfield** — Experimental, good for abstract/product focus
- Strengths: Creative control, product showcasing
- Best for: Product hero shots, closeups
- Use as: Supporting asset, not primary

**Sora** — Overkill for this use case (too expensive, too slow)
- Skip unless you have specific premium needs

### TIER 2: Asset Creation (Complementary)

**NanoBanana** — Social templates & captions
- Use for: Adapting video into static carousel ads
- Workflow: Video → NanoBanana cuts → static slides

**Creatify** — AI video ads with voiceover
- Use for: If owner doesn't want to appear on camera
- Alternative: AI avatar delivers testimonial (but less authentic)
