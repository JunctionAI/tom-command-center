## KLING.AI PROMPTS (Before/After Transformation Videos)

Use these exact prompts in Kling. Generate multiple variations of each.

### Prompt Set 1: Golden Retriever Limping → Running
```
Golden retriever limping slowly through backyard, difficulty jumping,
reluctant to play. Low energy, moving stiffly. Then: same dog 3 weeks later,
running freely and joyfully, jumping over low obstacles, playing fetch
with owner, high energy. Clear visible improvement in mobility. Before/after
style, 15 seconds total. Realistic motion, natural outdoor lighting, backyard
setting. Documentary style, not polished or overly produced. Show the contrast
between struggle and freedom.
```

### Prompt Set 2: Senior Lab with Joint Issues
```
Older yellow lab struggling to get up from lying down, walking slowly with
stiff gait, reluctance to climb stairs. Then: same lab 3-4 weeks later,
moving more freely, walking with better posture, able to play with family,
more active and engaged. 15 seconds. Natural home and outdoor settings.
Realistic pet movement. Show before/after contrast in energy and mobility.
```

### Prompt Set 3: Small Dog Struggling to Jump
```
Small dog (spaniel or similar) struggling to jump on couch, trying multiple
times with difficulty, limping slightly after attempts. Then: same dog
2-3 weeks later, jumping easily onto furniture, playful, energetic,
showing improved joint function. Indoor home setting. Before/after style.
15 seconds total. Emphasize the clear difference in confidence and ability.
```

### Prompt Set 4: Dog Playing vs. Avoiding Play
```
Border collie or similar active breed showing reluctance to play, slow
movements, sitting more than usual, avoiding fetch games. Then: same dog
after Pure Pets, running excitedly, playing enthusiastically, showing
improved agility and willingness to engage. Outdoor park or backyard.
15 seconds. Documentary style. Real natural lighting preferred.
```

**HOW TO USE IN KLING:**
1. Go to Kling.ai → "Create Video"
2. Paste prompt into description field
3. Set duration to 15 seconds
4. Click generate
5. Wait 3-5 minutes
6. Download as MP4

**GENERATE 2-3 VARIATIONS** so you have options for A/B testing

## SEEDANCE PROMPTS (Quick Alternative Variations)

Use Seedance for faster generation + multiple takes of similar concept.

### Prompt Set 1: Owner Testimonial + Dog Activity
```
A dog owner in home setting, holding Pure Pets supplement bottle,
showing product to their dog. Brief testimonial: "After 3 weeks, she
could jump again." Cut to dog playing, running, showing mobility.
Natural lighting, real home. 15 seconds. Authentic, not scripted.
```

### Prompt Set 2: Product Close-Up + Owner Message
```
Close-up of Pure Pets product (powder or supplement form), owner
showing what they give their dog daily. Owner speaks naturally:
"This changed our routine." Cut to happy dog showing improved
mobility. Home setting. 15 seconds. Conversational tone.
```

### Prompt Set 3: Before/After Owner Reflection
```
Owner at beginning: "My dog was slowing down." Then: same owner
with dog showing improvement: "Three weeks in, I couldn't believe
the difference." Show dog's improved activity. Home/backyard.
15 seconds. Authentic emotion, not over-produced.
```

**HOW TO USE IN SEEDANCE:**
1. Go to Seedance.com → Dashboard
2. Select "Create Video"
3. Paste prompt
4. Choose "testimonial" or "product showcase" template
5. Generate (faster than Kling, usually 2-3 min)
6. Do multiple takes of the same concept for variations
