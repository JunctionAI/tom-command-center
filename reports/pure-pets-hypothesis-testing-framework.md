**Evidence:** "Support Your Joints" = 52.85% email open vs "Support Your Health" = 30% open

**Test Design:**
```

VARIANT A:

VARIANT B:
- Headline: "Pure Pets: Dog mobility in 3 weeks"

**Why This Matters:**

**Win Criteria:**
- Highest CTR = highest relevance
- Test for 3 days, pause lowest performer by day 4

**Evidence:** Email playbook shows UGC + testimonials = 3x higher engagement than model photos

**Test Design:**
```

- Generic setting, high production value

VARIANT A:

- Home or park setting, authentic

VARIANT B:

**Why This Matters:**

- Real dogs are more relatable than models
- Meta's algorithm flags generic stock photos

**Win Criteria:**
- Real photos should have lower CPC (higher quality score)
- Expected 10-20% CPC reduction vs stock
