## RALPH MESSAGING FRAMEWORK

When you write copy for Ralph ads, use this structure:

**Headline Options:**
```
✓ "Meet Ralph: Our office dog's Pure Pets journey"
✓ "Ralph couldn't jump. Then came Pure Pets."
✓ "After 3 weeks on Pure Pets, Ralph was jumping again"
✓ "See how Pure Pets transformed Ralph's mobility"
```

**Body Copy Template:**
```
"Ralph is our office dog. For months, he struggled with mobility
and joint stiffness. Reluctant to jump, reluctant to play.

After 3 weeks on Pure Pets, he was transformed.
Jumping, running, playing with full energy again.

Pure Pets isn't just a supplement. It's a return to living fully.

See Ralph's transformation. Imagine what it could do for your dog."
```

**Why This Works:**
- Opens with relatability (our office dog = real, familiar)
- Shows specific problem (mobility, jumping)
- Quantifies timeline (3 weeks)
- Shows measurable result (jumping, running, playing)
- Uses emotional angle (return to living fully)
- Calls to action (imagine what it could do)
